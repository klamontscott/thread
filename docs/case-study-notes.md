# Thread — Case Study Notes

## Problem Statement
UX researchers spend 2+ days per interview round on manual synthesis. Existing AI tools produce surface-level summaries without analytical depth, evidence linking, or confidence assessment.

## Design Decisions
- Confidence badges: transparency about AI limitations builds trust
- Expandable quotes: evidence available on demand, not cluttering the primary view
- Executive summary first: optimized for the stakeholder sharing use case
- Open questions section: unique differentiator — AI identifies gaps, not just findings
- Warm palette: deliberate departure from clinical AI tool aesthetics

## Portfolio Narrative
Thread demonstrates:
1. AI product design thinking (prompt engineering as design)
2. Systems thinking (structured output schema, confidence modeling)
3. Full-stack implementation capability
4. Research methodology expertise embedded in the product
